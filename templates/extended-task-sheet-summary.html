How do extended tasks work?
WorkBreakthroughs
The team will need to earn "work" by completing tasks then rolling the effect dice if they succeed. ... or acquire "breakthroughs" as described below. The team will need breakthroughs.
Critical Failures on those tasks happen at - 20, and Critical Successes happen as normal for a task roll (i.e. include focus). Critical Successes and Critical Failures cancel each other out. After cancelling, if you have critical failures remaining, the task fails. However, for each critical success remaining, you earn a breakthrough.
Each task will have a difficulty of and a resistance of . (Resistance is applied by removing one success for each point of resistance when adding up successes.) If you have surplus critical successes OR you succeeded the task difficulty, roll the number of challenge dice based on the primary discipline PLUS 2 additional dice for that task.
Generally, there are no "effects" in play, so the dice results "with effects" will usually still only count as 1 success. (unless GM has a valid reason to make them worth 2). If the success total after all calculations is or more successes then you earn a breakthrough!
Yes, you can get a breakthrough for both Critical Success surplus AND the success total, thus earning multiple breakthroughs per attempt.
You succeed at the task when you complete either the work track or the breakthrough track.
